Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our apartments and services

What is the minimum stay requirement?

Our minimum stay requirement is typically 1 day. However, for certain peak seasons or special events, we may require a minimum stay of 2-3 days. Please check the availability calendar for specific dates.

What is included in the rental price?

Our rental prices include accommodation, utilities (water, electricity, internet), access to building amenities (swimming pool, gym, etc.), basic toiletries, and weekly cleaning for stays longer than 7 days. Additional services such as daily cleaning, airport pickup, and laundry are available at an extra cost.

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM, and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy is as follows:
- Full refund if cancelled 7+ days before check-in
- 50% refund if cancelled 3-6 days before check-in
- No refund if cancelled less than 3 days before check-in
Special circumstances may be considered on a case-by-case basis.
